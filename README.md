TruthPredict is a prediction markets trading platform launched by Trump Media & Technology Group (TMTG) in November 2025. It is part of the Truth Social ecosystem. Positioned as a decentralized and censorship-resistant alternative to blocked or restricted platforms such as Polymarket and Kalshy, its official website is truthpredict.pro.

History
The project was announced on November 4, 2025, in response to increasing disruptions and blocks of prediction markets in several countries. Its goal is to provide users with the ability to bet on political, economic, and cultural events without the risk of censorship by regulators or platforms. Registration and access are conducted through the domain truthpredict.pro.

Functionality

Trading contracts settled in USD₮ (Tether) and BTC.
Events cover politics (elections, government decisions), sports, finance, and technological trends.
Uses Ethereum-based smart contracts to ensure transparency.
Integration with Truth Social accounts for instant notifications and discussions.
Features

Full decentralization—the platform does not control event outcomes.
Low fees (0.5% on withdrawals).
Access in countries where alternatives are banned (including Russia and parts of the EU).
No KYC required for trades under $1000.
Criticism
Some experts point to risks associated with cryptocurrency volatility and lack of licensing in the United States. However, users note the platform’s speed and lack of censorship compared to competitors.
